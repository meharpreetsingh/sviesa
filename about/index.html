About us

Šviesa - The Photography & Media Club, SLIET

Founded by Nandan Kumar and Ripudamanbir Singh in 2016 as the official photography and movie club of SLIET. Sviesa has grown to become a family of 20+ members and is ever-growing. Initially, the club mainly focused on photography and videography to promote SLIET. But, now it has made itself versatile and expanded itself to become the official photography and media club of sliet. From clicking pictures to publishing its monthly newspaper. Sviesa is surely the best creative club in sliet and now it is planning to expand itself all over the country.

Why was ŠVIESA started?
Šviesa is an amateur level Photography and Movie club aimed at nurturing talent and setting standards in the art of photography mechanics and movie dynamics while exploring the possibility of photographs as a visual language.
